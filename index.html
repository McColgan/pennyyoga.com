"Teaching yoga that is mindful and beneficial for body, mind and spirit."

Who am i?

Penny is a qualified British Wheel of Yoga Teacher and offers personalised Yoga classes, meditation and relaxation techniques via 1-1 yoga sessions, group classes and yoga workshops to help you achieve a level of fitness, health and harmony. The yoga classes in Corby, Northampton & Peterborough are adapted to suit all levels from beginners to advanced. Penny is also training as a Yoga Therapist with Real Yoga so that she can help people feel better through yoga movement, breathing, meditation and relaxation.

Yoga Mummys

Mum's come and do yoga while babys/tots play. You can stop to soothe and feed as you need without feeling stressed. Available Thursdays 1.30 at Kingscliffe Underground centre (below Village Hall)

Younger People

Qualified and experienced to teach 1-2-1 yoga sessions, small groups and in Schools. Fully accredited to teach yoga for children and teens. Lots of experience of working with students with learning, physical and mental health issues.

Hatha Yoga

Penny's Hatha Yoga sessions help you develop a strong core, stronger muscles and more flexibility. My classes are perfect if you like to be mindful, peaceful and enjoy practicing in these quiet places (sometimes to great yoga music). Do you want to improve general fitness and wellbeing? Come along to get a chance to lesson stress through breathing and relaxation. All abilities made to feel welcome and valued.

Restorative Yoga

Penny is also a Relax and Renew (Restorative Yoga) yoga teacher helping people to benefit from yoga postures with bolsters and blocks to relax into. Delivered in a very gentle but beneficial form. Restorative Yoga Classes available in Peterborough on Friday morning. Book now to reserve your place or contact me for information about 1-1 or small group yoga sessions.

Short Yoga Programme

Penny is also offering short programmes of Yoga to care for backs and Yoga to lesson the effects of stress. In the summer you can also join regular meditation courses.
